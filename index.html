About Finvitt...

Today, it takes an average of 18 days to set up a business in India, down from 30 days in 2009. On the other hand, countries like New Zealand have a seamless process of business incorporation which takes place through a single-window via one agency. It just takes half-a-day with a single form and minimal cost to set up a business in New Zealand.

The cost of starting a business in India is astronomical, and the procedures involved can be daunting without local knowledge. There are 12 procedures to complete in the initial set up of a business costing 49.8% of income per capita. It takes almost a month (27 days) to complete the tasks on average, which is well above the OECD average of 12 days.
